# ⚡ Energy Consumption Dashboard

## 📌 Business Problem
Household electricity consumption can vary significantly throughout the day and across different periods.  
The goal of this project is to analyze usage patterns and identify opportunities to optimize energy consumption and reduce costs.

---

## 📊 Dataset

- Source: Household electricity consumption dataset  
- Data includes:
  - Date & Time
  - Power Usage (kW)
  - Voltage
  - Current

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze total and average energy consumption  
- Identify peak usage hours  
- Detect daily and weekly consumption patterns  
- Provide insights to improve energy efficiency  

---

## 🧠 Approach

1. Data cleaning and preprocessing  
2. Creation of time-based features (hour, day, month)  
3. Development of KPIs using DAX  
4. Dashboard design focused on usability and clarity  

---

## 📈 Key Metrics (KPIs)

- Total Consumption  
- Average Consumption  
- Peak Consumption  
- Consumption by Hour  
- Consumption by Day  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Energy consumption peaks during evening hours  
- Weekday usage is higher compared to weekends  
- Consumption patterns vary depending on time of day  

---

## ✅ Recommendations

- Shift high-energy activities to off-peak hours  
- Optimize appliance usage during peak times  
- Monitor consumption trends regularly
